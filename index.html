TimeFlow is designed with an offline-first architecture. All your data belongs to you and stays on your device.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or phone number) to external servers.

To provide core time-tracking features, the Application requires the following system permissions:

  • Exact Alarm Permission: Required to trigger precise widget updates and task status changes exactly when your time blocks end.

Privacy Policy for TimeFlow

Effective Date: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the privacy terms of our mobile application, TimeFlow ("Application"), created as an independent product.

1. Information Collection and Use

TimeFlow is designed with an offline-first architecture. All your data belongs to you and stays safely on your device.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or phone number) to external servers.
  • Task Data: All tasks, time blocks, and scheduling data created within the Application are stored strictly locally on your device's secure storage (SQLite database). We have absolutely no access to this data.

2. Permissions Used by the Application

To provide core time-tracking features, the Application requires the following system permissions:

  • Foreground Service & Notification Permissions: Required to display the dynamic timer on your lock screen and keep it ticking in real-time.
  • Exact Alarm Permission: Required to trigger precise widget updates and task status changes exactly when your time blocks end.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate any third-party tracking, ads, or analytics SDKs. Your behavior inside the app is completely private.
